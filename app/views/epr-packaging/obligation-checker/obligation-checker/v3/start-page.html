Check if the extended producer responsibility (EPR) for packaging regulations apply to you

Organisations that handle and supply (create, import, distribute or sell) packaging and packaged goods may need to pay a charge for managing their packaging after it has become household waste, under the EPR regulations.

They may also need to submit data about how they handle and supply packaging.

Use this tool to find out:

  • if the regulations apply to you
  • what charges your organisation may need to pay
  • what you need to do next to comply with the new regulations

You'll need to know:

  • your annual turnover
  • the ways that your organisation handles and supplies packaging
  • the total weight (in tonnes) of packaging you handle each calendar year
