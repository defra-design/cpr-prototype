What this means for your organisation

What you’ll need to do in 2023

{% if data["small-producer"] == 'yes' %}

You must record data about all the packaging you handle and supply through the UK market from 1 January 2023.

{% endif %} {% if data["small-producer"] == 'no' %}

You must:

  • record data about the packaging you handled or supplied through the UK market from 1 January 2023
  • register for the EPR online service (registration opens in October 2023)
  • pay a fee to cover the cost of the environmental regulator
  • submit data about the packaging and packaged goods you supplied or handled through the UK market during the 6 month period between January and June 2023 (submissions for this data open in July 2023 and close 1 October, 2023)
{% endif %}

What you’ll need to do in 2024

{% if data["small-producer"] == 'yes' %}

You must:

  • register for the EPR online service (registration opens in January 2024)
  • pay a fee to cover the cost of the environmental regulator
  • submit data about the packaging and packaged goods you handled or supplied through the UK market throughout 2023 (submissions for this data open in January 2024 and close 1 April 2024)
{% endif %} {% if data["small-producer"] == 'no' %}

You must submit data about the packaging and packaged goods you supplied or handled through the UK market during the 6 month period between July and December 2023 (submissions for this data open in January 2024 and close 1 April, 2024).

For any packaging that you handle and supply that is likely to be discarded by households, you must also:

  • pay a fee to cover the cost of the scheme administrator (the arrangements of this will be confirmed later)
  • pay a waste management charge (the system for paying your fees will open in April 2024)
{% endif %}

Your next steps

  1. Prepare to collect data about the packaging you handle or supply in 2023. Find out more about the data you'll need to collect.
  2. Create an account on behalf on your organisation. Once you have created an account, you’ll receive email updates about the registration process and when it opens.
