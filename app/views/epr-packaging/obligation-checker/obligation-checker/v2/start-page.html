Check if you need to register for the Extended Producer Responsibility for packaging scheme

Use this service to find out if your organisation or group will need to:

  • register for the scheme
  • submit data about how you work with packaging each year
  • pay annual charges to cover the cost of recovering, recycling and disposing of the packaging waste you produce

To complete this service you'll need to tell us:

  • your annual turnover
  • the ways that your organisation or group works with packaging
  • the estimate tonnage of packaging you work with each calendar year
It should take about 5 minutes to check if you are obligated to register.
