How much packaging in total did you sell, hire, loan, gift or discard in the UK in these ways, in 2022?

What do you mean by sell, hire, loan or discard?

For packaging to be classed as ‘sold’, it must have been made available on the UK market for end consumers.

If you have sold empty or filled packaging, but not to an end consumer, you do not need to include this in your calculation.

For any packaging you hire or loan out in the UK, you only need to include it in your total if it’s being hired or loaned out for the first time.

Discarded packaging is any packaging that you have imported into the UK and thrown away before selling.
