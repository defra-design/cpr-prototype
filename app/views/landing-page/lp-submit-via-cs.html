Compliance scheme: Compliance Co Scotland

What Compliance Co Scotland is responsible for

On behalf of Acme Org, Compliance Co Scotland is responsible for:

  • submitting packaging data to the environmental regulator
  • reporting organisation details and keeping them up to date
  • handling packaging recovery notes (PRNs) and packaging export recovery notes (PERNs) - these are evidence that packaging has been recycled, recovered or exported
  • paying fees and charges to the environmental regulator

What Acme Org is responsible for

Acme Org is responsible for any waste management fee that may apply to it.
