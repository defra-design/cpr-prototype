What are 'reporting periods'?

For the organisations Compliance Co works with, you need to report data that shows their packaging activities over a period of time. Each period of time is called a 'reporting period’.

For each data period, Compliance Co must report:

  • organisation details
  • packaging data

You may need to collect some information before the period starts, and you submit the data after the period has finished. This means you’ll have tasks for more than one data period at the same time.

There are different data periods for large and small organisations.

You’ll also need to handle packaging recovery notes (PRNs) and packaging export recovery notes (PERNs). These are evidence that packaging waste has been recovered, reprocessed or exported. They cover all packaging activities in a calendar year - they are not linked to specific data periods.
