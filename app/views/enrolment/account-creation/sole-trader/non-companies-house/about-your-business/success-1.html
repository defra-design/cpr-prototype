Your account permissions

You are now an 'administrator' for your organisation on this service.

This means you can prepare data for submission but someone with the permissions of an 'approved person' will need to submit it.

You can also be an 'approved person' if you are eligible.


Roles eligible to be an approved person

A person who has control or management of a business which does not have a registered office, head office or principal place of business in the UK.

Examples of job titles for this role include:

  • chief executive officer
  • owner
  • partner
  • company director or secretary
  • chair, treasurer, or secretary of an unincorporated organisation

More than one person can be an approved person. If you invite someone to be one, you will need to confirm their role, enter their name and contact details.


What happens next

You can either:

  • agree to be an approved person if you are eligible/confirm you are eligible to be an approved person
  • invite someone eligible to be an approved person
  • continue and do this later
