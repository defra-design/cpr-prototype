FE workforce data collection

Academic year 1 August 2020 to 31 July 2021

Before you start


Use this service to:

  • Submit your organisation information and staff details; the two parts to this collection.
  • Save each section and return as many times as needed before you submit.
  • Upload a file from your own internal system for your staff details or add them one at a time using the digital service.

The time it takes to submit will depend on how many staff you employ.

Start now

What you'll need to tell us

Your recruitment activity for:

  • senior and management vacancies
  • teaching vacancies
  • apprentices

Your staff details:

  • personal details
  • job role and level
  • qualifications and experience
  • contract and salary

We need to know about your own staff, not subcontractors or other third party employees.



Where to find your information

You may need to gather the data from these sources:

  • HR systems
  • MI systems
  • job specifications
  • job applications
  • CVs

The data collection is open for six weeks.
