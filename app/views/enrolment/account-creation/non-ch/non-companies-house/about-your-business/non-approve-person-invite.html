Add an approved person

You should invite an 'approved person' who can complete tasks on behalf of the organisation.

You do not have to do this immediately but one must be appointed before you can:

  • apply for accreditation
  • submit quarterly returns after registration

An approved person may delegate their function to another person (a delegate) at a later stage - subject to regulator approval.

More than one person can be nominated - you will need to confirm their role, enter their name and contact details.


Roles eligible to be an approved person

A person who manages or controls an unincorporated association or organisation without a registered office in the UK. For example:

  • Chief Executive Officer
  • Director
  • Owner
  • Partner
