Add an approved person

You are eligible to become an approved person. An approved person needs to perform tasks within the account on behalf of the organisation.

More than one person can be nominated - you will need to confirm their role, enter their name, email adress and phone number.


Any of these roles can self-nominate or nominate one of these roles as an approved person:

  • director
  • partner
  • company secretary
  • a relevant person who has control or management of an unincorporated association
  • a person who has control or management of a company with no registered office in the United Kingdom

Approved person nominations can be changed in Manage account
