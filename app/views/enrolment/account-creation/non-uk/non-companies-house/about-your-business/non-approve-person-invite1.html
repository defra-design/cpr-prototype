Add an approved person

You can continue to add and register your organisation - but if you want to apply for accreditation, an 'approved person' will need to be in place.

This person will be able to complete the tasks below:

  • apply for accreditation
  • submit quarterly returns after registration

You will need to confirm their role and enter their contact details.

More than one person can be invited. You can do this now or later.


Who is eligible to be an approved person

A person who has control or management of the business. For example:

  • Chief Executive Officer
  • Owner
  • Partner
  • Company Director or Secretary
  • Chair, Treasurer, or Secretary of an unincorporated organisation
