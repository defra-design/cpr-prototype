Guidance Public register for the ‘report packaging data’ service

On this page you can search for registered reprocessors and exporters, download documents for registered producers, compliance schemes, reprocessors and exporters, and find organisations that are subject to enforcement action.

For anything before reporting year 2024, use the Public Register of Registered Producers on the National Packaging Waste Database (opens in new tab) .
