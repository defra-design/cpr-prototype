Check the list of large organisations on the ‘report packaging data’ service

Check the list of large organisations on the ‘report packaging data’ service.

For some packaging activities, this will help you work out who is responsible for reporting data. You may also need a record of this information for audits, or questions from the environmental regulator.

On the National Packaging Waste Database, this is called the ‘Public Register of Registered Producers’.
What is a ‘large organisation’?
On the ‘report packaging data’ service, an organisation is ‘large’ if it:

  • has an annual turnover of £2 million or more
  • is responsible for supplying or importing more than 50 tonnes of empty packaging or packaged goods in the UK

Who needs to use this

Use the list if you do any of the following activities:

  • import packaging that contains goods into the UK to sell
  • own an online marketplace that allows non-UK organisations to sell packaged products in the UK
  • supply packaging as empty

If you’re unsure, check the section on packaging activities in the ‘Packaging data: what to collect’ guidance.

How it works

Search the list for organisations you work with when you supply packaging in the UK as part of the above activities.

If they’re on the list, they are responsible for reporting about that activity. If not, your organisation is responsible.
