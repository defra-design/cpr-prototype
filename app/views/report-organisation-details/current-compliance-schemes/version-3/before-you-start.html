Before you start

1. Read the guidance

Find out how to report organisation details (opens in a new window). There are template documents you can use to build your files.

The guidance includes:

  • the information you'll need to provide
  • template CSV files you can use
  • how to complete the CSV files

1. Prepare your files

You will need to prepare CSV files to submit. You can open and complete CSV files in any spreadsheet program.

Use the templates provided or create your own. If you create your own CSVs, they must exactly match the templates.

Your organisation details file includes:

  • address
  • packaging activities
  • the people who'll be verifying and submitting your data

You may also need to report your organisation's:

  • brand activities - if you supply goods to the UK market in primary, secondary, or shipment packaging under their own brand
  • partners - if you're registered as a partnership in Companies House

You'll need to record each of these in a separate file and submit them at the same time as your organisation details.
