Accept this PERN against your 2025 obligation?

This PERN relates to waste received in December 2025. It can count towards your 2024 or 2025 obligation.
Accepting it will credit 100 tonnes towards your 2025 obligation.
I want to accept it against my 2024 obligation
Follow these steps:
  1. Open this PERN.
  2. Select ‘reject this PERN’ at the bottom of the PERN.
  3. Ask the issuer of the PRN to issue a new PERN against your 2024 accreditation.
  4. After they've issue it, sign in to NPWD and accept it. This will accept it against your 2024 obligation.
