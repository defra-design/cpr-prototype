Accept this PRN against your 2025 obligation?

This PRN relates to waste received for reprocessing in December 2024. It can count towards your 2024 or 2025 obligation.
! Warning Accepting it will credit 100 tonnes towards your 2025 obligation.
I want to accept it against my 2024 obligation
Follow these steps:
  1. Go back to the PRN.
  2. Select ‘reject this PRN’ at the bottom of the PRN.
  3. Ask the issuer of the PRN to issue a new PRN against your 2024 accreditation in NPWD.
  4. After they've issued it, sign in to NPWD and accept it.
Go back to the PRN to reject it.
