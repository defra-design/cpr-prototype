Payment for your application

The total tonnage of PRNs you plan to issue determines the charge for your application. This total is for all the materials you plan to issue PRNs on. For example, if you were planning to issue 200 tonnes of paper PRNs and 201 tonnes of plastic PRNs, this would mean the charge for issuing over 400 tonnes of PRNs applies.
