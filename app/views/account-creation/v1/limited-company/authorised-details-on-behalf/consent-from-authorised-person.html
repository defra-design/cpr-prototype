We need consent from an authorised person

To approve your account, the regulator needs consent from an authorised person in your organisation.

The authorised person must one of the following:

  • director
  • company secretary
  • partner

If you’re not an authorised person

If you’re not an authorised person, you can still create an account.

You also need to tell us the contact details of an authorised person at your organisation. We’ll contact them to approve the account.

You need to know your authorised person’s:

  • full name
  • role - director, company secretary or partner
  • email address
